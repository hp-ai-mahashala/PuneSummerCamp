📅 Programme Schedule

3 Days of Pure Awesomeness 🔥

01
🤖 AI & The World
Tuesday, 3rd June 2025
9:00–9:30🎉

Welcome & Ice Breaker

Get to know your fellow AI explorers! Fun games, introductions, and a sneak peek at what's coming over 3 days.

9:30–11:00🌍

AI Around Us – Introduction to Artificial Intelligence

What is AI? Where do we see it? From Netflix recommendations to voice assistants — AI is everywhere! Interactive quiz and hands-on demos included.

11:00–11:15🍎

Break Time!

Snacks, stretch, and chill. You've earned it.

11:15–1:00💬

Talking to AI – Prompt Engineering Basics

Learn the magic of prompts! Why does "tell me a story" give different results than a smart, detailed prompt? Practice hands-on with AI tools and discover the secrets of great prompting.

1:00–2:00🍱

Lunch Break

Recharge for the afternoon session!

2:00–3:30🏆

Prompt Challenge – "Best Prompt" Competition!

Teams compete to write the most creative, effective prompts. Judges (the instructor 😄) will score on creativity, clarity, and results!

3:30–4:00🌟

Day 1 Recap & Reflection

Share your biggest aha moment. What surprised you most about AI today?

02
🎮 Build. Code. Play.
Wednesday, 4th June 2025
9:00–9:30🔁

Morning Warm-Up & Day 1 Revisit

Quick quiz on yesterday's content — what do you remember? Prize for the fastest hand up!

9:30–11:30🐱

Scratch Game Design – From Idea to Game

Meet Scratch! Learn how to use blocks, sprites, and backgrounds to build your own game. Start with templates, then unleash your creativity.

11:30–11:45☕

Break

Grab some water. Save your progress!

11:45–1:00🎨

Design Thinking Workshop

Before building anything great, you need to think like a designer. Learn the 5 stages: Empathize → Define → Ideate → Prototype → Test. Then apply it to your game concept!

1:00–2:00🍱

Lunch Break

Fuel up — the afternoon gets exciting!

2:00–3:30🕹️

Build Your Game – Hackathon Style!

Teams of 2–3 build and complete their Scratch game. Instructors roam around to help. Final 15 mins: demo your game to the whole class!

3:30–4:00🌟

Game Show & Wrap Up

Each team presents their game. Vote for the most creative, most fun, and most innovative! Winners announced.

03
🌐 My Website, My World
Thursday, 5th June 2025
9:00–9:30🔁

Morning Warm-Up

Speed round: "What is HTML? What is AI? What is a prompt?" Buzz in to answer — fastest wins a star!

9:30–11:00🌐

Build Your Website – Introduction to Web Pages

What makes a website? Learn the basics of HTML, images, links, and layout. Each student starts their personal website about something they love!

11:00–11:15☕

Break

Almost there, champions!

11:15–1:00✨

Make It Beautiful + AI-Assisted Design

Use AI tools to help write content for your website! Add colours, fonts, and images. Make it uniquely, authentically YOU.

1:00–2:00🍱

Lunch Break

One more session to go — the grand finale!

2:00–3:00🚀

Publish & Showcase Your Website

Share your URL with classmates, family, and the world. Your website goes LIVE today! Share it on the gallery below.

3:00–4:00🎓

Closing Ceremony & Certificate Distribution

Celebrate 3 days of learning, building, and growing. Group photo, certificates, and memories to last a lifetime! 🎊
